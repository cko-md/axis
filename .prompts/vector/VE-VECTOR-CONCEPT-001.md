## Prompt

Create a high-resolution product-design concept sheet for a premium personal
operating system module named VECTOR Arcade. Show three clearly distinct lobby
layout concepts side by side: Instrument Deck, Archive Console, and Panoramic
Bay. Also show two card families: a large mechanic-focused Mission Plate and a
compact save-state Continue Strip. Include one mobile adaptation and one warm
light-mode adaptation as smaller insets.

Visual language: dark cold cinematic Axis dashboard, warm bone typography areas,
precision hairlines, restrained brass/gold accent, graphite and deep navy glass,
instrument-panel discipline, editorial industrial design, subtle atmospheric
depth. Retro-futurist but sophisticated and readable. Avoid purple neon AI
dashboard styling, childish arcade motifs, fake CRT scanlines, chromatic
aberration, pixel body text, decorative switches, giant rounded card soup, and
generic terminal styling.

Every cover image must visibly suggest a game mechanic: hidden time-memory,
precision platforming, orbital gravity puzzle, glider flight, ecosystem lab,
miniature town, or voxel arena. Leave clean negative-space plates where real DOM
titles and metadata will sit. Do not render readable product names, game titles,
small UI copy, logos, or nonsense text inside the image; use abstract typographic
blocks and icon shapes only. Controls shown must look functional, not ornamental.

Presentation: polished UX concept board, straight-on screens, consistent scale,
crisp details, no device mockup hands, no brand imitation, original artwork.
